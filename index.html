Continuous Target Speech Extraction: Enhancing Personalized Diarization and Extraction on Long Recordings

Submitted to ICASSP 2024

Authors: Anonymous

Abstract: Target speaker extraction (TSE) is mainly aimed at the short audios with high overlap and the existence of target speakers. It cannot be directly applied to complex real-world scenarios. In this study, we propose continuous TSE (C-TSE); which mainly consists of pBSRNN and TSVAD. We use a simple Pre-processing fusion approach which shows good results and also propose a fusion approach. To enhance its utilisation of long continuous speech, we introduce multi-head self-attention to TSVAD. In addition to this, we use a combination of evaluation metrics such as INT, DER, SI-SNR,etc to objectively evaluate the TSE ability. Experiments show that C-TSE further improves the processing of target speaker absence and speech activity detection while maintaining the high speech separation quality of the original TSE. It push TSE into continuous long recordings with more speakers and variable overlap ratios.
